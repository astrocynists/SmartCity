Latest Smart City Additions

Smart Street Lights Installed in Sector 5

Based on multiple citizen requests from local residents in Sector 5, solar-powered smart street lights have been installed. These lights automatically adjust brightness depending on pedestrian movement and time of night, improving safety and reducing energy usage.

Completed: March 15, 2025

EV Charging Stations Launched in Main Market

Shop owners and daily commuters suggested the need for electric vehicle charging hubs in high-traffic zones. Responding to their feedback, 4 EV stations are now live and operational in the central parking area of Main Market.

Completed: April 2, 2025

Raj Patel, Field Technician

"We installed 120 smart streetlights this month alone! Each one saves enough energy to power a home for 2 days."
